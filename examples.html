Rainbow 6 Update. Patch Y5S1.2

Buck, Goyo, Jager, Mozzie and Ying operators have been balanced. slight gun balancing to Caveira's M12 and Kaid and Goyo's TCSG12. fixed players cliping inside of the excavator on Oregon, Fixed other visual issues and game bugs.

Buck was given Frag Grenades instead of Claymores, Increased Skeleton Key Magazine. Goyo was given 1 less Volcan shield. Jager is now a 2 speed 2 armor operator. Mozzie was removed of his Super Shorty shotgun secondary. Ying now has increased Candelas to 4 and replaced claymores with smoke grenades, Increased the T-95 LSW damage to 46.
