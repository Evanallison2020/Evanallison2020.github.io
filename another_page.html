Valorant

June 1st 2020

Valorant taken out of the beta phase and put into full release for anyone to play.

New Agent in Reyna, New map in ascent. With buffs to Jett and Phoenix and Nerfs to Omen and Sage. New mode with Spike Rush which gives the game a little more action.

Valorant

In Depth. Version 1.0 Update.

Multiple of Sage's abilities were changed. Her healing orb cooldown was changed to 45 seconds and her Barrier Orb health was reduced from 1000 to 800 per segment. Raze's blast pack was changed, with its damage radius now being 1 meter instead of 2 meters. Jett was significantly buffed where they changed her smoke duration from 4 to 7 seconds and her Tailwind ability breaks Cyphers Trapwires. Phoenix was buffed significantly aswell where his Blaze ability duration was increased from 6 to 8 seconds. Blaze damage was changed and the healing was changed. Omen was changed with making multiple of his abilities theoretically useless.
